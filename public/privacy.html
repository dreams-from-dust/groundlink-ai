Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

Welcome to GroundLink AI ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, protect, process, and handle your data when you use the GroundLink AI Grounded Knowledge Engine and document retrieval assistant.

` `

1. Information We Collect

` `

To provide high-quality Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and document grounding services, we process the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: When you authenticate via Google Sign-In or other credential providers, we receive your email address, name, and profile photo, processed securely via Firebase Authentication.
  • Uploaded Documents: GroundLink AI allows you to upload text-based files, guides, manuals, and transcripts to build a private knowledge corpus.
  • Query & Chat Data: We process the text queries you send to the assistant to locate relevant document passages and synthesize responses.
  • Vector & Embeddings Cache: We generate and cache multi-dimensional semantic vector representations of your documents to enable immediate local context search.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and safeguard your private, isolated document collections.
  • To parse, slice, and index your uploaded files to execute semantic similarity lookups.
  • To synthesize contextual answers using advanced language model logic.
  • To maintain local secure caching to bypass network roundtrips and protect data confidentiality.

3. Data Protection and Confidentiality

We implement strict state-of-the-art security measures to keep your data safe:

  • Isolation: Your uploaded documents and knowledge chunks are bound specifically to your authenticated User ID. No other user can access, query, or view your files.
  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your browser and our server is secured using robust TLS (HTTPS) encryption.
  • Safe Deletion: Clicking "Clear Database" or resetting your workspace permanently and immediately purges all associated documents and vector chunks from our active indexes and Firestore database storage.

4. Integration of Third-Party APIs (Gemini)

GroundLink AI leverages Google Gemini models to compute rich vector embeddings and generate precise natural language answers. When you perform a grounded search:

  • Only the specific matching text passages (the context chunks) along with your active question are sent to the Gemini API securely.
  • Your original unprocessed documents are never fully shared with third-party providers.
  • Data sent to the Gemini API is subject to standard enterprise privacy terms and is not used to train public foundational models.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have full, autonomous control over your data in GroundLink AI:

  • Right to Erase: You can purge all uploaded documents and vectors at any time using the in-app "Clear Database" controls.
  • Account Deletion: You can request complete termination of your authenticated access session.
